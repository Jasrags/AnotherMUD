# Aardwolf — Clans System

Source: https://www.aardwolf.com/mud/clan.html
Fetched: 2026-06-09

## Overview

Clans are a group of players following a common theme or goal and are a very important part
of Aardwolf.

## Clan Features

Clans have many features not commonly found among other worlds:

- **Recall room** — own dedicated recall point
- **Jail** — clan jail for disciplining members or prisoners
- **Donation room** — shared clan donation pool
- **Bank account** — members can deposit gold; optional tax rate to raise income from members
- **Clan-only skills and spells** — exclusive abilities
- **Clan transporters** — fast travel within clan holdings
- **Healing rooms / hospitals** — clan-exclusive healing
- **Clan halls** — physical player-built spaces with all features above
- **Guards** — purchasable guards to defend the hall
- **Mazes** — clan can build mazes a raider must penetrate to break into the hall
- **Clan equipment** — armor, potions, transporters
- **Clan morgue** — where members' corpses go upon death

All features are purchased through member gold donations or clan tax.

## Clan PK

Clans defend their halls from other clans through the PK system. Raiders must navigate
purchased guards and mazes to break in.

## Design Notes

- Clan economy is player-funded (gold donations + tax system)
- Physical infrastructure (halls, guards, mazes) creates PK-driven territorial gameplay
- Specialized features (morgue, hospital, transporter) make clan membership practically
  valuable, not just social
- Clan-only skills create exclusive mechanical incentives beyond roleplay
