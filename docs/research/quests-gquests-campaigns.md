## Quests

- Handed out by the Quest Master NPC
- Objective: find and kill a specific mob
- Reward: Quest Points (QP)
- Solo only — players forbidden from receiving outside assistance
- QP can be saved and used to purchase special Aardwolf Quest Equipment (very powerful,
  often with permanent haste, sanctuary, etc.)

## Global Quests (GQuests)

- Similar to quests but competitive — multiple players race to complete the same targets
- Declared randomly throughout the day for different level ranges
- Players opt in when a GQ of their level range is declared
- Objective: kill a specific number of target mobs within a time frame
- First player to kill all targets wins quest points
- Areas with GQ targets respawn at a faster rate during the GQ to give all participants a
  fair chance

---

## Campaigns

- A campaign involves killing multiple targets within an 8–12 hour real-time limit
- Rewards: quest points; occasionally trivia points, training sessions, practice sessions, gold
- Targets are typically higher level than those for quests or GQuests
- Progress is saved if you quit; timer keeps counting down (real time, not game time)
- One campaign per level — must level before taking another
- If you level while on a campaign, you immediately get a new campaign at the new level
- If you skip multiple levels mid-campaign, you only get one new campaign for current level

## Design Notes

- Three escalating quest formats: solo (Quest), competitive (GQuest), long-form (Campaign)
- Campaign timer is real-world, not game-world — creates real urgency
- Quest points bridge the quest and economy systems (can buy gear upgrades)
