# BatMUD — Features Overview & Guilds

Source: https://www.bat.org/help and https://www.bat.org/help/guilds
Fetched: 2026-06-09
Online since: April 14, 1990

---

## Overview

BatMUD is a pure text LPMud that has been online continuously since 1990. Average 300+
players online, peak 450+. Constantly evolving — described as "more than first impressions
suggest."

---

## Feature Highlights

- **44 races** — 31 immediately selectable; rest available through rebirth stages or by
  invitation (player-run races)
- **100 levels** with special titles
- **Reincarnation / Rebirth** — re-spec including race change; available at and after max level
- **Extremely diverse skill and spell system** — correlates with character background,
  chosen guilds, and secret societies
- **Detailed combat system** with partying (grouping) and raiding (party linking) and
  combat statistics
- **Player-to-player commerce** — tailored guilds and carts to facilitate trade
- **Player-built items, castles, and cities**
- **Seafaring and shipwrights** including sea battles
- **Ridable mounts**
- **Customizable pets**
- **Hundreds of custom zones** with items of varied powers and bonuses
- Entire world on a **single outerworld map** — gives a true sense of travel between areas
- 13,000+ rooms

---

## Character System

- 5 backgrounds: Civilized, Evil Religious, Good Religious, Magical, Nomad
- 30+ guilds derived from backgrounds
- Racial guilds for each race (separate from background guilds)
- Multi-background guilds available
