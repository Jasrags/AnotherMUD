## Getting Started

You begin in **Town Square, in Hearthwick Village**.

Worn cobblestones spread out beneath your feet, polished smooth by generations of foot-traffic. A weathered notice board leans against the side of the well at the square's centre. Streets lead off to the north and east, and the south road runs out toward open country.

Paths lead north to Hearthwick Forge, south to The Village Gate, east to Market Row.
