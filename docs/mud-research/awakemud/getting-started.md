## Getting Started FAQ

### Connect

- Telnet: `play.awakemud.com` port `4000` (see `features-overview.md`).
- Discord community for help.

### Essential starting commands

| Command | Use |
|---------|-----|
| `commands` | View all available actions |
| `help` | Access help topics (e.g. `help newbie`) |
| `score` / `balance` | Check carried cash |
| `phone` | Communication (must be turned on) |

### Core mechanics (how things resolve)

Success relies on hidden dice rolls using attributes or skills. Higher ratings
increase success chances and "degrees of success." Tasks are evaluated against a
**Target Number (TN)** — lower TNs are easier. Modifiers like smartlinks reduce
TN; poor conditions raise it. (Full math in `combat-numerics.md`.)

### Starting resources & money

- **Taxi:** free until 100 **TKE** (total karma earned) and graduating the
  Neophyte Guild.
- **Initial jobs:** three NPCs in the Neophyte Guild offer starter work to build
  reputation.
- **Cash vs. credstick:** Johnsons pay in carried cash; credsticks are needed for
  legal purchases.
- **Credstick acquisition:** buy at Circuit Couriers (Auburn) or Nybbles and
  Bytes (West Tacoma); must activate before use.

### Key locations

| Place | Where |
|-------|-------|
| Ammo | West, south, west from the Neophyte Guild exit |
| Beano's Collection Agency | West Tacoma, across from Coffin motel |
| Pawn Shops | Little Chiba (Seattle's Japantown) |
| ATMs | Neophyte Guild; Velasquez Bank (West Tacoma); UCASBank (Seattle NE) |

### Beginner tips

- Take notes on Johnson locations for future reference.
- Johnsons rotate job offerings roughly every two real-time minutes.
- Reputation requirements are per-job, not per-Johnson.
- Use the taxi to explore and collect grid coordinates.

## Roleplaying Run FAQ

Player- and staff-hosted roleplay runs, rewarded by the RP immortal.

### Key points

- You **don't** need to know Shadowrun 3rd Edition rules.
- Runners aren't paid from personal funds — the RP imm reimburses all rewards.
- Participants earn syspoints, karma, nuyen, and other rewards.

### Definitions

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Prun** | A roleplaying shadowrun session hosted by players |
| **ImmRun** | A roleplay session hosted by staff / RP imm |
| **Event** | Any non-session activity (tea parties, corpse-dump outings, etc.) eligible for rewards |

### Flags & commands

- `TOGGLE HIRED` — signal you're occupied with RP during a Prun/Immrun.
- **Questor flag** — request from RP imm; hides your character name from emotes
  for scene-setting (abuse → revocation/punishment).

### Payment & rewards

- **Before running:** contact the RP imm to agree on reasonable reward amounts
  and secure funding (prevents exploitation).
- **After running:** post your log to the `#rp-logs` Discord channel for review.
- **Rewards:** players get negotiated nuyen/karma/syspoints; hosts get equal
  nuyen/karma plus bonus syspoints; potential NERP items, news reports, world
  alterations. The RP imm verifies effort/length, not performance quality.

### RP log formatting

- Look at each character once at session start (identification).
- Keep banter; remove large off-topic tangents; retain dice rolls.
- Disable client echoes; use `TOGGLE PROMPT` for cleaner logs; bulk-edit with
  regex (Notepad++) if needed.
