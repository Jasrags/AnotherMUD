## Overview

Character creation uses the Shadowrun 3rd Edition **priority system**: five
priority slots **A–E** are each assigned to one of the five categories
(Attributes, Skills, Resources, Magic, Metatype). Each category's letter
determines the points/nuyen/options available. See the per-archetype example
builds below for concrete A–E assignments and their resulting values.

### Creation restrictions (from House Rules)

- Attribute / Skill / Equipment Rating limit at chargen: **6**.
- Availability limit: **8**.
- Cyberware set to worthless during chargen (free Essence-Hole repair via
  explant/reimplant).
- Restrings: 5 complimentary.

## Step 1 — Pronouns

Choose **Male / Female / Neutral**. "All this affects are the pronouns NPCs use
for you, and what the game represents you as."

## Background Helper

A structured questionnaire for developing a character backstory/concept
(Shadowrun, 2064, SR3). Answer 15 core questions:

1. **Birthplace & upbringing** — where the character originated.
2. **Family background** — parents' occupations, marital status, siblings.
3. **Family of origin** — whether the character has children.
4. **Education** — how and where they learned their skills.
5. **Livelihood** — primary occupation beyond shadowrunning.
6. **Beliefs** — political, spiritual, religious alignments.
7. **Morality** — ethical boundaries regarding violence.
8. **Long-term goals** — in-character objectives and agendas.
9. **Motivation** — reasons for the shadow lifestyle.
10. **Character flaws** — significant weaknesses.
11. **Hatreds** — groups or behaviors they despise.
12. **Hobbies** — interests beyond their work.
13. **Names** — birth name vs. shadow identity (street name).
14. **Best achievement** — most positive action taken.
15. **Worst action** — regrettable decisions made.

Plus five additional reflection prompts covering heroes, fears, and attitudes
toward death.
