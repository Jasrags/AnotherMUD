## Class identity

Myrddraal (a.k.a. "Fades" / "Lurks") are an **advanced remort class** —
"more powerful incarnations of trollocs and darkfriends that have been rewoven
into the pattern by the Dark One" (classes overview). They are a **humanoid race
open to play via Remort**, and also exist throughout the world as mobs.

> "Myrddraal are a humanoid race open to play (via Remort) as characters.
> Additionally, Myrddraal mobs can be found all over the world."

Achieved through special quests involving "deep game knowledge, leadership,
strategy and warfare."

### Prac advantage

Across the base-class skill matrices, the **Myrddraal trains every base-class
skill at cost 1** (warrior, hunter, and rogue skills all = 1 prac per session) —
the broadest cheap-training reach of any class.

## Myrddraal as mobs (context for the playable race)

The mob behavior describes what the remort class can do:

> "Myrddraal mobs are the principle form of darkside combat support throughout
> the world. They are found heavily in darkside cities, fortifications, and as
> the leaders of patrols throughout the map. **Trolloc clanmasters are
> Myrddraals.** Shaidar Haran, a notable Myrddraal, is found in the city of
> Thakan'dar."

Combat traits (from the mob description):
- **Quite strong in combat.**
- Able to **fear** their opponents.
- Wield **black swords** which can **poison** opponents.
- Will **occasionally change targets** in combat to engage channeling female
  channelers they were not engaged upon.
- When killed, **their corpse remains behind to continue the fight.**
- Often **escape combat near death by fading away.**
