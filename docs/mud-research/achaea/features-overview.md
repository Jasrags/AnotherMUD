# Achaea — Game Features Overview

Source: https://www.achaea.com/game-features
Fetched: 2026-06-09
Last Updated by site: June 1, 2026

Achaea is a text-based RPG where you can fight, craft, trade, sail, roleplay, or run a city,
all in the same persistent world. Running since 1997, published by Iron Realms Entertainment.

---

## Major Systems

### Combat (PvP & PvE)
- Hunt denizens for XP and gold (PvE)
- Player vs Player combat: thousands of abilities across 21 classes
- Curing system: afflictions stack, player tunes their own curing responses
- Triggers and combat systems (Mudlet/Nexus scripting)
- Dragonhood: reach level 99, travel to Mhojave Desert, awaken dragon soul
  - Six elemental affinities: poison green, sonic silver, flame red, acidic black,
    lightning blue, psychic gold

### Roleplay & Living World
- Roleplay-encouraged world
- Characters have persistent history, reputation, and relationships
- Cities rise and fall on player decisions
- Gods watch and intervene
- The world remembers what you do — favors and feuds echo for years

### Quests & Exploration
- Two continents: Sapience (starting) and Meropis
- Dozens of islands and several other planes of existence
- Quests range from short errands to multi-week multi-stage puzzles
- Fellowship of Explorers for thorough explorers
- Most quests start by greeting a denizen and reading for clues

### Crafting — 16 Tradeskills
(See crafting.md for full detail)
Armoursmithing, Artistry, Augmentation, Beverages, Conjuration, Cooking, Furnishing,
Gathering, Harvesting, Inkmilling, Jewellery, Remedies, Shipfitting, Tailoring,
Toxicology, Weaponsmithing

### Player Economy
(See player-economy.md for full detail)
- Fully player-run: players produce, price, and sell
- Mining with Legions (defend/attack mines)
- Player shops (limited slots = status marker)
- Importation and commodity trading at Delos market

### Cities, Great Houses, Divine Orders
- Six player-run city-states
- Players can lead cities: set policy, wage war, run espionage
- Great Houses: player-led organizations
- Divine Orders: join a god's order, raise shrines, fight on battleplane of Nishnatoba
- Elections for city leadership

### Seafaring
- Five oceans: Borean, Chelic, Eusian, Notic, Sefyric
- Ship types: Windcutter (fast/nimble), Thalassian Seastrider (cargo), War Galley (combat)
- Naval combat and piracy
- Sea trade routes

### Player Housing
(See housing.md for full detail)
- Build room by room; write own descriptions for every room
- Place and hide exits, add doors
- Furnish with player-crafted goods (Furnishing tradeskill)
- Hire customizable servants (butler, maid, cook, steward, page)

### Classes & Races
- 21 classes, each with 3 skill sets and 100+ abilities
- 14 playable races
- Dragonhood as late-game achievement (level 99)

---

## Notable Design Choices

- Economy runs entirely on players — no fixed NPC prices on key goods
- Shop scarcity (limited slots per city) makes shopkeeping a status marker
- Crafting is a support profession for combat (curatives, venoms, weapons)
- Housing costs paid in commodities, not just gold, linking it to the economy
- Roleplay is encouraged but not enforced
